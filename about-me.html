Hi, I'm Kayla

Always laugh when you can, it's cheaper than medicine.

I am self-taught & community taught designer and developer. I have always had a passion for design and art and also happen to enjoy mathematics too. In seventh grade a family friend suggested that I study architecture in university. After a little bit of research I fell in love with the idea of being an architect.

Toward the end of my university career I had a change of interest and did not want to pursue architecture but still enjoyed design. After some time of figuring out what was next for me, a friend suggested going into coding because it is a growing industry and pays well too. Coding is so broad but after some research and networking I decided on web development. I started a boot camp, then through a personal networking I was able to be apart of this amazing year-long apprenticeship where I learned full-stack development alongside other developers and working on projects.

Now I freelance and work with local small businesses to help them bring their presence online with designing and developing their website. I get to manage some of them too!
